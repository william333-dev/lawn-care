About us

Two local mates who love a tidy job.

Crossman & Mackay is Sam & Ashton — two hardworking Aucklanders who started out to do lawn care and property maintenance the right way.

We genuinely enjoy keeping properties looking their best — whether it's a quick mow, a full garden clean-up, or clearing out the gutters before winter.
